ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN (엔하이픈; also stylized as EN-) is a seven-member multinational boy group under Belift Lab. Formed through the reality show I-LAND, they debuted on November 30, 2020 with their first mini album Border : Day One. The name of the group was described as "just like hyphens connect different words to make new meanings, the members will connect, discover each other, and grow together"

Enhypen, a product of the competitive survival show I-LAND, is a South Korean boy group that has captured the hearts of fans worldwide. Their music, a blend of various genres, showcases their versatility and talent. The group consists of seven members: Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. Each member brings a unique charm and skill set to the group, contributing to their overall dynamic and appeal.

Enhypen's music is characterized by a contemporary and edgy sound, often incorporating elements of hip-hop, R&B, and electronic music. Their songs, which frequently explore themes of youth, growth, and self-discovery, resonate deeply with their fans. With their powerful vocals, intricate choreography, and visually stunning music videos, Enhypen has established themselves as one of the leading fourth-generation K-pop groups.

Enhypen has formed a strong bond with their fans, known as ENGENE. The group is known for their genuine interactions with fans through various platforms, creating a sense of community and belonging. Their dedication to their craft and their fans has solidified their status as one of the most beloved groups in the K-pop industry.
