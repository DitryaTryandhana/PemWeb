ILLIT

ILLIT (아일릿; also stylized as :ll:+) is a five-member multinational girl group under Belift Lab. Formed through the JTBC reality show R U Next? and originally as six, they made their debut on March 25, 2024 with the mini album Super Real Me. Their group name is derived from the phrase "I Will Be It". It is a group name that combines the autonomous and proactive will (I will) with the pronoun (It) that signifies something special. Depending on the verb that fits between the two words, it can become anything.

ILLIT is a South Korean girl group formed by Belift Lab, comprising five talented members: Yunah, Minju, Moka, Wonhee, and Iroha. The group made their official debut on March 25, 2024, with their first mini-album, "Super Real Me," and the title track "Magnetic."

ILLIT was formed through the survival reality show "R U Next?", a joint project between HYBE Labels and JTBC. The show aimed to find the most promising female trainees to form a new girl group. The members of ILLIT were selected based on their exceptional vocal, dance, and performance skills. With their bright future ahead, ILLIT is sure to continue making waves in the global music scene.
