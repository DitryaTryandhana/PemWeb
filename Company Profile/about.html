ABOUT

BELIFT LAB (빌리프랩, also known as BE:LIFT) is a South Korean media company. It was founded as a joint venture between CJ ENM and HYBE in 2018 where CJ ENM held the controlling stake at around 52%.On August 10, 2023, it was announced that HYBE signed a deal to for CJ ENM's entire 51.5% stake of Belift Lab, thereby gaining complete ownership of the label.

BELIFT LAB is a dynamic entertainment company founded on September 17, 2018, as a joint venture between CJ ENM and HYBE Corporation. The agency is dedicated to discovering and nurturing new talent in the K-pop industry. BELIFT LAB is known for its innovative approach, combining expertise from various fields such as media, branding, education, and IT to create a sustainable future for K-pop

The agency's mission is to provide fans with the best possible experience while maintaining a healthy relationship with their artists. BELIFT LAB aims to expand artist IP beyond traditional boundaries, creating a new paradigm for K-pop and redefining entertainment. Their roster includes popular groups like ENHYPEN and the newly debuted ILLIT. BELIFT LAB is best known for forming and managing the boy group ENHYPEN, which debuted in 2020 through the survival reality show "I-LAND." The group has since gained significant popularity both domestically and internationally.

The agency aims to create innovative content and artist management strategies by combining the expertise of its parent companies in media production and K-pop artist development. BELIFT LAB is committed to innovation and is always on the lookout for passionate individuals to join their team, contributing to the creation of captivating music content and IP-based businesses that resonate with fans worldwide
